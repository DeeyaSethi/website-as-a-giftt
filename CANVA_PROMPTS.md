## How to Use:

1. **Go to Canva** ‚Üí Create new design ‚Üí Custom size (1920x1080 or 16:9)
2. **Click "Apps"** ‚Üí Find "AI Image Generator" or "Magic Media"
3. **Paste one of the prompts above**
4. **Generate** ‚Üí Choose the best result
5. **Download** as PNG or JPG
6. **Upload to Imgur** or **Google Drive** (get public link)
7. **Paste the image URL** in the "Custom Music Thumbnail" field
